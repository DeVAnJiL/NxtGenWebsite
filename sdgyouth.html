SDG Youth Forum




SDG Youth Action Forum is designed to help young leaders with skills and competencies to effectively lead actions and initiatives for the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) is a global agenda of 17 goals, 169 targets, and 232 indicators that aim to end poverty, promote economic growth, preserve the environment, uphold peace and justice, and establish partnerships by the year 2030. The SDG Youth Forum aims to:

  • 1. Introduce the SDGs to targeted young participants.

    • 2. Solidifying commitment among the participants to act for the United Nations’ 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development.

    3. Showcase current commitment of young leaders to act for the United Nations’ 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development.

    4. Develop respective effective,impactful,and sustainable action plans to act and contribute for the Sustainable Development Goals.
