TEDxTribhuvanUniversity


TEDx Tribhuvan University is the first independent TEDx event taking place inside Tribhuvan University. TEDx talks are influential talk programmes from expert speakers on education, business, health, science, technology, creativity, etc. The main objective of the programme is to unleash fresh ideas from local communities. TEDx Tribhuvan University is independently organized under a free license granted by the TED community. The event will be conducted under the guidelines of TED on format, speaker and curation, audience, event organization and more. NxtGen is a proud licensee of TEDx. We are highly motivated to bring such a reputable programme to our local audiences. This programme will help our audiences and viewers to develop new thinking and strategies to better their own life and contribute to society.
